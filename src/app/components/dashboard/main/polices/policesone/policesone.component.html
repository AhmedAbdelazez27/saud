Privacy and Security

Saud bin Rashid Al Mualla Charitable and Humanitarian Est. is committed to protecting the rights of all visitors to this portal. We are committed to maintaining the confidentiality of sensitive information, and we have prepared this security and Privacy declaration to disclose our approach to the collection and dissemination of information on this web portal.

Collection and use of electronic information: Our server automatically captures your IP address when you visit the portal. (Your Internet Protocol address is your computer's unique identifier that allows other devices connected to the internet to send data, but without identifying you personally).

Our use of your IP helps us to diagnose problems in our server and to perform the necessary statistics to measure the use of the portal. We do not allow anyone outside the technical team of Saud bin Rashid Al Mualla Charitable and Humanitarian Est. to access your IP. To provide better service and efficiency, certain sections of this portal use cookies as part of our interaction with your browser.

Cookies: A cookie is a small text file sent to your hard drive to enable our server to recognize your computer. Cookies do not send information from your hard drive, and we do not combine information collected through cookies with personal information to determine your identity or email address.

When you visit certain parts of this site, you may be asked to provide personal information (such as name, address, and gender) required to complete a transaction. In any case, you are invited to visit our portal and benefit from the valuable information it offers.

Secure transmissions: Please be assured that this portal has security measures in place to protect the information you provide to us.

Personal information protection: Your personal information will only be available to association employees who need access to it. Such information will not be available to the public without your consent and will not be traded, sold, or exchanged with any third party without your prior consent.

Access to this information is restricted to qualified professionals providing services to the association.

The right to review, modify, or correct your data: Users providing personal information have the right to review, modify, or correct their data.
